About the App

Wortsch. is an intuitive flashcard app that helps you learn German vocabulary effectively through a smart spaced repetition system. Perfect for students preparing for A2 and B1 German exams.

Key Features:

  • Curated vocabulary sets for A2 and B1 proficiency levels
  • Smart spaced repetition system
  • Visual progress tracking
  • Bidirectional learning
  • Support for English and Russian translations
  • Customizable learning experience

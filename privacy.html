Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Last Updated: 2.03.2025

Summary

Wortsch. does not collect or transmit any personal data. All data, including your learning progress and preferences, is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers or shared with third parties.

Introduction

Fedor Galkov ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Wortsch. mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

We designed Wortsch. with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process your personal data on our servers.

Information Collection and Use

Data Stored on Your Device

Wortsch. stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Learning progress (words you've learned, difficulty levels)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Session history

This data is stored solely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Analytics and Crash Reports

Wortsch. does not use third-party analytics services. We do not collect crash reports or usage statistics.

Third-Party Services

Wortsch. does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect your data.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 ("Children").

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of collecting personally identifiable information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Deleting Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete your data by:

  • Using the reset function within the app
  • Deleting the app from your device

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
